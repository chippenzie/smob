Scandalous!

Charity on November 21st, 2008

Or maybe not so much.

This is why I am always a day or three behind on posting things that spread like wildfire on the internet. I happen to strongly dislike making an ass of myself with incorrect information, so I don’t.

The story, if you can’t watch video: Liberal bloggers, TV commentators, and even Jon Stewart, made a big deal out of a clip that showed world leaders not shaking hands with President Bush.

“Bush Snubbed!” “Bush Has Cooties!”

Turns out, Bush already shook all their hands. The video goes on to show lots of friendly interactions with President Bush and world leaders.

Oops!

This happens so often, I am going to need to start a category for it.
